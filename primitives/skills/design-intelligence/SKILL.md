# Design Intelligence

**Trigger:** "design philosophy" | "semantic layout" | "taste"

## The Core Loop

1. **Composer (Architect)**: Creates the **Semantic Layout Tree** (Content & Logic only).
2. **Stylist (Decorator)**: Applies **Design Tokens** & **Philosophy** (Mood & Tone).
3. **Critic (Linter)**: Validates against **Design System Contract** (Constraints & Rules).

## The Three Lenses of Taste

1. **Fashion**: Attitude, Silhouette, Materiality (Sharp vs Cozy).
2. **Print**: Typography, Grid, Optical Alignment.
3. **Digital**: Interaction, Statefulness, Motion as Explanation.

## Key Directives

- **Think in Trees**: Decouple intent from implementation.
- **Use Tokens**: `surface.base`, `text.primary`, `space.4`. No hex codes.
- **One Voice**: Consistent typographic hierarchy and mental model across Web/Native/TUI.
