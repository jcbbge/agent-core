# Cognitive Approaches

**Trigger:** "how should I think about this" | "mental models"

## The Principles

### 1. Feynman Visualization
> "Don't power through errors. Figure out how to visualize or intuit what you're doing. Make it obvious."

**Application:**
- Transform abstract → concrete
- Transform static → dynamic  
- Transform linear → spatial

### 2. Hamming Constraints
> "Feel out the points of greatest constraints. These are your points of greatest leverage."

**Application:**
- Where is the system most rigid?
- What's the rate-limiting step?
- Where does small effort → large result?

### 3. Taoist Flow
> "Don't fight the system. Figure out what the universe wants to do, then ride and shape it."

**Application:**
- What does the system naturally want?
- How to shape vs. dam the flow?
- Where is energy already moving?

### 4. Cognitive Faculties
> "Give agents the faculties of mind: ability to image, draw, work iteratively."

**Application:**
- Enable "sketching" or drafting
- Iterative refinement loops
- Intermediate representations
